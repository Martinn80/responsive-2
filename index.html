About Me

I am Martin I am 39 years old. I am married and I have a 2 year old daughter. My background is mainly in trouble shooting. I was an aircraft technician for 3 years in Oakland, CA and I also worked in the maritime industry for about 6 years doing everything from a deckhand, engineer, and boat captain here in the SanFrancisco Bay. And I had a few jobs in between, everything from construction work to custodial work. I began my coding journey in 2017 the day my wife told me we were having a baby. Since 2017 I have been teaching myself how to code. Of course once our daughter was born coding took a backseat to the the full time job and a newborn baby, but I studied when I got the chance. Now my baby is 2 years old and there is no better time than the present to take things up a notch in my coding journey and commit to a life changing coding bootcamp!!

I'm excited for this class and for this opportunity. I look forward to working with my classmates and building lifelong relationships.
